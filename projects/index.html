Janne Swaegers

I mainly do research along the following topics and questions:

Evolutionary consequences of range expansion

How do populations evolve through the process of range expansion?

Range expansion and hybridization

What are the factors that lead to heterogeneity in genomic divergence between hybridizing species?

To what extent is adaptive introgression aiding thermal adaptation in range expanding species?

Thermal plasticity and adaptation

To what extent are responses to global warming a consequence of the result of an interplay between evolution and phenotypic plasticity?
